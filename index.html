HawkerToGo

Description

For my Full Stack Development Project, my team created a website called HawkerToGo. It is a food delivery website where users can order hawker food from different hawker stores, and have them delivered to their doorsteps. This website was created to provide a platform for hawkers to make their presence online, giving them more exposure to a larger customer base, allowing hawkers to grow their businesses. For my part, I was in charge of the user and account management. Users can register and verify their accounts via email using SendGrid API, view and update account details and delete their accounts. Users can also choose to change their passwords when they are signed into their accounts. When users forget their password, they can reset their passwords via email using SendGrid API.

Technology

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, NodeJS, MySQL, SendGrid API

ImOkApp

Description

For my AI Applications Project, my team decide to collaborate with NYP School of Health and Social Science (SHSS) to create a health monitoring app called ImOkApp. It is an android application made for tablets using Kotlin to allow the elderly patients to track their own health by monitoring their metrics such as weight, blood pressure and bmi, making sure they are in good health and their health metrics are in normal range. If their health metrics are not in the normal range. The app will alert the user and provide health advice, or alert the user to seek help from a healthcare professional in worse case scenarios. This app was created not to replace healthcare professionals but to help them ease the load, so that they can spend more time with the patients.

Technology

Kotlin
